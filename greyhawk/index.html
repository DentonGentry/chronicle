Other Passages

The same gaming group initially played in a campaign set in the Shadow World of Kulthea. That game is now in permanent hibernation, though the Chronicles from it remain online. Edwin the Loremaster from the Kulthean campaign served as inspiration for the Edwin of this campaign, but is otherwise unrelated.

Interspersed with the Kulthean campaign the group played through the Tomb of Horrors, though no Chronicle of this adventure was kept. The same gaming group subsequently played through The Return to the Tomb of Horrors, set in the world of Greyhawk.

After RTOH the group played in a different campaign, The Unravelling, which has now concluded.

Interspersed with the Unravelling the group played in another campaign of intrigue involving ancient races of snake-men, but no chronicle of that campaign is available online.
