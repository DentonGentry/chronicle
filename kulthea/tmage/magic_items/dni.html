Adhesive DNI jack

  A very small hemispherical device with a peel-off adhesive backing on the flat side and a Coalition-standard DNI jack on the spherical side. When the backing is removed and the device affixed to the operator's forehead the enchantment is activated, magically linking the user's nervous system to the DNI jack. It can be put to any purpose an implanted jack can, including interface to a cyberdeck or to powered armor. Note that this device does not confer any relevant skill to allow operation of these systems, it merely provides access. Once activated the DNI connection lasts for 5 minutes per level of the technomage who enchanted it.

Weight: 0.1 lbs

Spells used: Arcane Embed II (5), Interface I (2)
Time to enchant: 4 days
