Technomagic Items

  Part alchemist and part systems technician, the technomage is capable of constructing and enchanting items. The technomage must construct the item to be enchanted via conventional means, and items enchanted by a technomage tend to be less powerful magically than items created by an alchemist of the same level. The enchantments which may be placed on an item depend on the complexity of the item's aura.
  A few representative items are presented here. Not everything described below is enchanted, some items presented are purely technological but result from the unique perspective of a low-tech culture catapulted essentially overnight to vast technological knowledge.

  Players in Marc's game should note that these are just ideas, not everything listed here has been run by Marc for approval.
