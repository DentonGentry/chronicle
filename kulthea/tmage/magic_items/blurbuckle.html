Blur Projection Buckle

  A large buckle, complete with attachment hardware for a leather belt, made to be worn about the waist. The buckle contains a miniature holographic emitter which has been enchanted to cast a Blur spell when activated. The Blur adds +10 to the DB of its wearer. The buckle contains a small power cell which must be recharged after each usage.

Weight: negligible

Spells used: Arcane Imbed II (5), Blur(1)
Time to enchant: 4 days
