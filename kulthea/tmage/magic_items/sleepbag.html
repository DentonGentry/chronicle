Bag of Sound Sleeping

  A variable-insulation polymer sleeping bag of standard Coalition military issue. A control panel at the head of the bag has a readout of the current time, temperature, humidity and other atmospheric conditions. The control panel can adjust the insulation level provided by the polymer bag to suit the ambient conditions, and can also be set to awaken its occupant at a specific time via audible alarm.

  The control panel has been enchanted to cast a Sleep spell, keyed to affect the occupant of the bag. The effect is to allow the occupant to sleep normally despite the discomfort of wearing metal armor, or other conditions which would prevent normal sleep. Each spell cast drains a charge from the attached utility cell.

Weight: 3 lbs

Spells used: Arcane Embed II (5), Sleep V (1), Drain Charge (2)
Time to enchant: 18 days
