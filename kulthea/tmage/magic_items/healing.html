Healing Wand

  A small metal box inscribed on all sides with runes. A standard utility cell is contained within the box, with its contacts welded to the interior. The cell and wand are enchanted with an Arcane Healing spell, which will drain one charge each time it is cast.  Because the utility cell is permanently affixed to the wand it cannot be recharged through conventional means. Spells on the Arcane Circuit spell list can recharge the utility cell.

Weight: 0.5 lbs

Spells used: Arcane Embed I (3), Healing II (1)
Time to enchant: 9 days
