1. Research - Allows caster to research special imbedding spells to be used in creating items. [TC p66]

2. Cantrip Imbed - Allows caster to imbed minor special effects into an item. Cold fire running along a blade, crackling energy at the end of a wand, etc are within the range of effects afforded by this spell. The GM may allow minor functionality from these effects such as starting campfires, but they are more often used to command a higher price for a magic item via impressive bluster.

3. Arcane Imbed I - Allows caster to imbed a 1st level spell from the Arcane realm in an item or potion. [similar to TC p66]

4. Empathy - Allows caster to enchant an item with "empathy". [TC p71]

5. Arcane Imbed II - As Arcane Imbed I, except that 2nd level spells from the Arcane realm or 1st level spells from another realm can be imbedded.

6. Daily I - Allows caster to imbed a 1st level Arcane spell (using an Arcane Imbed spell) that can be cast once per day. [similar to TC p66]

7. Arcane Imbed III - As Arcane Imbed I, except that 3rd level spells from the Arcane realm or 2nd level spells from another realm can be imbedded.

8. Low Intelligence - Allows caster to enchant an item with "low intelligence". [TC p71]

9. Arcane Imbed IV - As Arcane Imbed I, except that 4th level spells from the Arcane realm or 3rd level spells from another realm can be imbedded.

10. Daily III - As Daily I, except 1st to 3rd level spells can be imbedded; a 1st level spell could be cast 3 times per day; a 2nd or 3rd, once per day.

11. Arcane Imbed V - As Arcane Imbed I, except that 5th level spells from the Arcane realm or 4th level spells from another realm can be imbedded.

12. Daily IV - As Daily I, except 1st to 4th level spells can be imbedded; a 1st level spell could be cast 4 times per day; a 2nd twice per day; a 3rd or 4th, once per day.

13. Arcane Imbed VI - As Arcane Imbed I, except that 6th level spells from the Arcane realm or 5th level spells from another realm can be imbedded.

14. Medium Intelligence - Allows caster to enchant an item with "medium intelligence". [TC p71]

15. Arcane Imbed VII - As Arcane Imbed I, except that 7th level spells from the Arcane realm or 6th level spells from another realm can be imbedded.

16. Daily VI - As Daily I, except 1st to 6th level spells can be imbedded; a 1st level spell could be cast 6 times per day; a 2nd 3x/day; a 3rd twice per day; a 4th, 5th, or 6th, once per day.

17. Arcane Imbed VIII - As Arcane Imbed I, except that 8th level spells from the Arcane realm or 7th level spells from another realm can be imbedded.

19. Daily VIII - As Daily I, except 1st to 8th level spells can be imbedded; a 1st level spell could be cast 8 times per day; a 2nd 4x/day; a 3rd or 4th twice per day; a 5th, 6th, 7th or 8th, once per day.

20. Arcane Imbed X - As Arcane Imbed I, except that 10th level spells from the Arcane realm or 9th level spells from another realm can be imbedded.

25. Daily X - As Daily I, except 1st to 10th level spells can be imbedded; a 1st level spell could be cast 10 times per day; a 2nd 5x/day; a 3rd, 4th or 5th, twice per day; a 6th, 7th, 8th, 9th, or 10th, once per day.

30. Arcane Constant - Allows a spell of 1st to 10th level to be imbedded (using an ArcaneImbed spell) so that it functions constantly.

40. Very High Intelligence - Allows caster to enchant an item with "very high intelligence". [TC p71]

50. Arcane Imbed True - As Arcane Imbed I, except that any level spell can be imbedded.
