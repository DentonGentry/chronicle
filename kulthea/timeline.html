Adventure Timeline

1 day Blackhawk recruits Sigmund, Urk, and Frisbee in Shakarnoth. J'uroch joins them and they briefly detour to a monestary of the Golden Path, and thence to Sigmund's family farm to retrieve the Ground Effect vehicle, which Vlad repairs.
1 day Travel to Coalition base where a rupture in space-time has occurred. Kill a Brachana, drag its carcass to a deep cave to wait out a sudden flowstorm.
1 day Wait out flowstorm
1 day Travel back to Shakarnoth, Brachana loaded on outbound cargo transport.
1 day Meet with Edwin again, assigned new mission to keep the Stone of Skaroth out of the hands of the Unlife. Elaine joined the group.
7 days Travel overland via car across Emer to Samblin. Car hidden 1 mile outside of Samblin
7 days Sea voyage to Jaiman, sped significantly by a ride on the back of a sea serpent. Docked in Sharneth, purchased horses.
4 days Travel overland across Fulcrumian peninsula. Stopped to visit Urk's mother.
7 days Arrived in Mopheus, summoned a flying boat of the Navigators. Flew across the Sea of Fire
1 day Camp on southern end of Aranmor. Ambushed by pirates, one (Pessrel) is captured while the rest are killed. Elaine disappears.
1 day ambush Aroth and remaining crew of the Siren as they cross a stone bridge. Porgno frees Pessrel during the night. Explore obsidian tower.
3 days Journey overland through swamp to Terek'Knev
4 days Explore Terek'Knev. Urk turned to stone and restored. Pyreforge freed from entrapment, joins party. Sasfiry and Chantille freed from magical imprisonment by Vrama-Ver, join party. J'uroch killed by demons. Black Cross arrives in nick of time for final battle with Porgno. Mount Kadaena erupts, lava envelops Terek'Knev. Stone of Skaroth too large to recover, a tactical nuclear weapon is used to destroy it.
3 days Journey back to Mopheus in Black Cross's speeder. Shizlink found hiding in speeder.
1 day Discover Edwin's shop in Mopheus. Formally initiated into the Ascension.
1 day Meet Ninghal Agorawal, convince her to return to Zor. Captured by Marleth, told of Enkin's imprisonment in Bazilar.
4 days Sea voyage to Bazilar. Attacked by Black Ship.
2 days Investigate Bazilar. Frisbee and Sigmund help Brothers of Kuar dispose of those who fell prey to the Sickness. The rest of the party met Zek, and then Ranix.
1 day Met Baldo the Brick and Oozer. Freed Enkin and Leneth from Coalition garrison.
21 days Rest, recuperate and train at Edwin's shop. Prophet joins group.
3 days Party at Gathering of the Ascension, await arrival of High King. Sigmund buys magic armor. Travel to First Pale.
14 days Recover amulet from mansion on the First Pale (only 1 subjective day passed on the Pale). Black Cross killed in combat with the Unlife. Frisbee in coma from serious wound.
1 day Return to Gathering, which had been attacked. Bury the dead and help the living. Sigmund buys another suit of magic armor. Rolfo joins group, it is his destiny after all.
1 day Recuperate at Edwin's shop in Bazilar. Crystal implanted in Frisbee's forehead, brings her back to consciousness.
1 day Invited to Enkin's palace for the night. Al-Cur joins group.
15 days Journey across Rhakaan to Zor.
1 day Travel across the wasteland to the Sisters of the Sacred Moon, find Ninghal.
1 day Travel to the Sacred Spring. Learn of Krenic, the line walker, who can locate the Heart of the World. Linea joins group.
1 day Find Krenic, who takes lots of cool toys away but provides directions to Heart of the World.
3 days Travel to the Heart of the World. Kick some Unlife ass.
3 days Travel back to the Sacred Spring.
18 days Journey across the wasteland and Rhakaan back to Bazilar.
