Persons of Note

Alathea Agorawa Now deceased, was pledged as sword sister to Ninghal Agorawa.
Aroth T'Kal Captain of the Siren and leader of a group or pirates marooned on the island of Aranmor a few years before the party arrived there. Aroth ordered a group of pirates to ambush the party, and was later killed.
Baldo the Brick City Engineer in Bazilar, responsible for the maintenance of the extensive underground sewer system. Baldo knows more about the secret ways under the city than anyone with the possible exception of Oozer. Baldo disappeared during the mission to free Enkin from the dungeons of Bazilar. No one knows his fate.
Banesh Sword Sister in the clan led by Ninghal Agorawa. Banesh is the best tracker amongst the Sisters of the Sacred Moon.
Bill the Halfling Rolfo's friend. Traumatized by his experience on one of the far Pales, he no longer speaks.
Black Cross An Althan and former member of the Coalition Expeditionary Forces (an elite unit of shock troops), Black Cross left the service and fought to free her world. Black Cross was killed on the First Pale.
Captain Carl In an incredible coincidence, the captain of every sailing ship the party has ever hired has been named Carl. [1] [2]
Dorlin,"the mouse" One of Ranix's men, part of the resistance against the usurper of Bazilar. Dorlin was severely injured during the mission to free Enkin, but recovered.
Drewton Current leader of the Fulcrumian fiefdom.
Edwin A Loremaster. As the primary patron of the adventuring party, Edwin rates his own page.
Enkin Rightful ruler of Bazilar after the death of his father Roake. Freed by the party from the dungeons under the Coalition garrison in Bazilar where he was being held by his uncle the usurper, Enkin eventually triumphed and is the current ruler of Bazilar. Enkin wed Leneth, a telepathic princess of Darakon, who had also been imprisoned by the usurper.
Erona Usurper of the leadership of Bazilar after the death of Roake.
The High King An ancient High Elf, leader of the Ascension against the Unlife.
Jahad A member of the crew of the Siren, shipwrecked on Terek'Knev with Aroth T'Kal. A bezerker, he died after Aroth ordered an ambush of the party.
Jarrian Current Emperor of Rhakaan, the most prosperous kingdom in Jaiman. Enkin of Bazilar seeks an alliance with him against the Unlife.
Jeewhiz Dragonette who lived in the Emerald Forest with Chantille many centuries ago.
J'uroch A monk of the Golden Path, and one time mentor of Frisbee. J'uroch is mute, speaking only to Frisbee via sign language. J'uroch was killed in Terek'Knev.
Kaelic An innkeeper in Bazilar.
Keledon Sigmund's mentor in the Midnight Brotherhood.
Kitacti An Ordainer demon released by the party from his captivity by the Unlife on the First Pale. Kitacti's presence on the First Pale was destroyed in a battle with the Unlife.
Kothar Londgard Proprietor of the Singing Frog Sanctuary in Bazilar. Frequent companion of Ninghal Agorawa.
Krenic A Line Walker in the wasteland of Zor, one who follows ley lines amassing arcane energy.
Leneth Princess of Darakon, a technologically advanced planet four jump points away from Kulthea. Darakon is threatened by the forces of the Unlife. Leneth was sent to Kulthea to form alliances with the forces of Light, but was captured by Erona and held in the dungeons under the Coalition garrison in Bazilar. She is highly telepathic, and looks perfectly human save for her jet-black skin and pupils. She later married Enkin after Bazilar was freed.
Leneya A Dreamwalker in the Mothers of the Sacred Spring.
Lentin Archbishop of the monastery of the brothers of Kuar outside of Bazilar.
Marleth Commander of the Coalition garrison in Mopheus.
Maska'Et Assists Piladon in handling the flying ship Neghiri.
Mathilda Ever-present attendant to Loremaster Edwin. Mathilda is a cursed shapeshifter, switching from one form to another every few minutes. The forms she assumes are always living organic, but may be any species of plant or animal. It is not known what race Mathilda was originally or how she came to be a shapeshifter. It is also not known if Mathilda was originally male or female, though now she can shift to either sex. There is a picture of Mathilda on Edwin's page.
Nadala A Sword Sister of Zor, and current leader of the Mothers of the Sacred Spring.
Ninghal Agorawa A Sword Sister of Zor, and current leader of the Sisters of the Sacred Moon. Ninghal was pledged to Alathea as sword sister; Alathea was killed some years ago.
Oozer The multiscanner determined that Oozer is human, but you wouldn't know it from looking at him. Oozer is completely covered in hair and filth. He can communicate with rats and prefers their company. He has spent his entire life in the sewers under Bazilar and can navigate through any portion of it with ease.
Penderack A central figure in Althan history, who united the religious and warrior castes under common leadership. He preached a philosophy of non-violence.
Pessrel A member of the crew of the Siren, marooned on Aranmor several years ago. Pessrel was one of a group sent by Aroth to ambush the party the first night after their landing on Aranmor. Pessrel was captured and tied to a tree. He was later cut loose by Porgno. Somewhere in there Pessrel fell in love with Urk... the natural 100 on Urk's Seduction roll might have something to do with it. Pessrel was not encountered again after Porgno freed him and might still be alive on Aranmor, though an erupting volcano and the detonation of a tactical nuclear weapon nearby make it exceedingly unlikely.
Piladon High Elf Navigator of the flying ship Neghiri. Piladon is always dressed in an ancient, ornate suit of full plate. His only assist in handling the Neghiri is another High Elf, Maska'et.
Porgno A poison using Magent, part of the crew of the Siren marooned on Aranmor. Porgno followed the party into Terek'Knev and assembled the armor of Tilek the Defiler. He was possessed by the spirit of Yogun, and later killed by the party.
Pyreforge Thane of Perdition and Bishop of the Third Carnal Pit, Pyreforge is a demon of Perdition (beyond the Pales). The party released him from his imprisonment on Aranmor, where he had been held by Vrama-Ver. Pyreforge later returned to Perdition due to a power struggle there, a struggle in which he ultimately prevailed.
Ranix Part of the leadership of the underground resistance to the rule of Erona in Bazilar. Ranix was formerly Captain of the Guard under Roake. After Enkin recaptured the city Ranix was reinstated.
Roake Father of Enkin, brother of Erona, Roake was the ruler of Bazilar until his death from the Sickness. Enkin had been abroad studying, but was summoned to Bazilar upon his fathers death.
Sessilak The enforcer and head of secret police in Bazilar while Erona was in power.
Tanada A bard of the Mothers of the Sacred Spring (one of the clans of the Sword Sisters of Zor).
Teleron A priest of Kieron and survivor of the massacre at the Gathering of the Ascension.
Telvan A member of the Midnight Brotherhood, and one of Sigmund's former teachers.
Terogoustrad An enormous demon in the form of a door which guards the entrance to Terek'Knev on Aranmor. Terogoustrad is presumed destroyed, either by the volcano which was erupting or by the tactical nuclear weapon Black Cross lobbed at the city when the party left the island.
Tilek Vrama's greatest champion, killed in the battle with the Army of Light 500 years ago. The Armor of Tilek the Defiler was amongst the most powerful artifacts created by mortals.
Tolkemak Shizlink's mentor.
T'Salini Lieutenant in command of (and the entire staff of) the Coalition depot at Gateway Station, on the border of the wasteland of Zor. Lieutenant T'Salini was affected by powerful mind destruction magic and is now a blithering idiot.
Vrama-Ver Last Queen of the Nereti and daughter of Kadaena, Vrama-Ver was nearly destroyed by an Army of the Light 500 years ago at the city of Terek-Knev in Aranmor. The party finished the job.
Yogun Father to Vrama-Ver and an alchemical genius. Yogun forged the armor of Tilek, binding his own soul into it. Yogun's soul was banished when Porgno was defeated.
Zek Head librarian of the Coalition library in Bazilar, and leader of the underground resistance to the rule of Erona. Zek was infected by the Sickness in Bazilar; it is not known how long he has to live.
Zol Half orc cyborg, works as a bartender at the Randy Sea Serpent near the fishing docks in Bazilar. There is evidence that Zol is an informer for Erona and/or the Unlife
