The Narrator
  Chantille looked at quite a few of the items in the party's possession while the ring was active. It would be tedious to list them all in the main body of the Chronicle. However, since one never knows what information might be relevant in the future everything is described here.
  • The 14" metal rod had a slight glow at one end.
  • The silvery strands from the necroborgs had a purplish energy coursing through them.
  • The Horn of Siege appeared as a gigantic, beautiful horn with a complex series of baffles inside.
  • The Arcane Power cell looked like a glob of energy which was slowly sucking additional energy from the air.
  • Prophet's armor looked like a rectangular box of translucent shields (presumably the runes which the fairies painted on it).
  • The 12" tablet found in the field of rubble had a strange electricity crackling over it.
  • The crystal decanter and plate found in the secret room off the crypt both had silver cords leading off into the ether; Chantille was unable to determine where they led.
