Force Field of the Unlife

The image was rendered using POV-Ray, a free raytracer available on the Internet. The Power Macintosh version of POV-Ray was used. Models and scene files used to create the image are available in zip format. The rough hewn stone texture used on the far wall was developed by Charles Fusner, and several other textures used in the scene are from the POV texture library.
