Self Guided Arrow (SGA)

also known as AFF (Arrow, Fire and Forget)

  In appearance the SGA is a fat arrow with a hemispherical, metallic tip. In actuality the Self Guided Arrow is a sophisticated, electronically controlled, self-propelled ordinance whose delivery mechanism is a traditional longbow. When fired the guidance electronics activate the Waveguide spell embedded therein, which creates a magnetic field extending from the missile to its intended target. The microcomputer controls the motions of the fins at one end of the shaft, keeping the projectile on a straight flight path using a pitot tube and magnetic sensors fitted in the tip. One second after launch the solid fuel booster rocket in the tail of the projectile is ignited, increasing its speed to approximately one half of the speed of sound. Upon impact the shaped charge warhead is detonated, sending shrapnel into its target.
  Due to the high velocity and guidance system, the Self Guided Arrow attacks on the SG12 firearms table.

Weight: 2x normal arror weight

Spells used: Waveguide (3)
Time to enchant: 1 day
