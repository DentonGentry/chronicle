Tech Famulus (V)

  A Famulus is a wizard's assistant, introduced as a training package in the Arcane Companion. Serving as a Famulus to a powerful technomage tends to develop one's skills using technology, and is represented by substituting in tech skills instead of Magical Lore.

Time of Acquire: 42 months
Starting Money: normal + 1d10 (open-ended)
Special:
  Expert system (+10 to Lore*Magical category) 30
  Expert system (+15 to one Magical Lore) 30
  Expert system (+10 to one Obscure Lore) 30
  +1 spell adder 40

  Choice of up to 2 skills - 2 (total)
Science*Technical skill category - 4
  Choice of up to 2 skills - 4 (total)
Power Awareness Skill category - 2
  Choice of up to 2 skills - 2 (total)

  Service (Valet) - 1

Stat Gains: none
