The Kulthean Campaign

This page contains supplementary material for a Rolemaster game run by Marc Mangus. The campaign was set on Kulthea (of Shadow World fame) which was conquered five hundred years ago by an interstellar empire called the Coalition. This campaign is now on permanent hiatus.

The Chronicle

Our group keeps a record of its adventures, told as a story. Here are the episodes thus far, and the date of the latest substantial update to their chronicle.
Episode I Knee Deep in the Dead 1/14/98
Episode II Demons of the Burning Night 3/17/98
Episode III Oh, The Places You'll Smell 3/18/98
Episode IV Smells Like Plasma, Tastes Like Chicken! 6/28/98
Episode V 'Til They Glow 5/1/99
Interlude I Indecisions of State 5/3/99

Marc runs the campaign as an epic saga, with certain sub-plots running through the adventures. Some of the unanswered questions from these subplots are listed on a separate page. Notable items recovered in the adventures are described, as well as important persons encountered. There is also a rough timeline of the adventures. Loremaster Edwin, the main patron of the party, has his own page.

Chronic!

Even Chroniclers need a distraction sometimes. Thus is introduced Chronic!, tales of adventures which are almost completely but not quite entirely wrong.
Chronic 1.0 There's a Hole in My Bucket 9/21/98
Chronic is a spoof of some of the Chronicle tales, and will not make sense unless one is familiar with the original.

Other Passages

The same gaming group subsequently played through The Return to the Tomb of Horrors, set in the world of Greyhawk. The group then moved on to The Unravelling, which has also now concluded.

The group is now playing in a new campaign set in the world of Greyhawk.
