Raytracing


Both images were rendered using Bryce 4. Ferranifer herself was posed in Poser 4 before being imported into Bryce. Her dress is from the Zygote Costume Shop CD. The chair she is sitting on is also from Zygote. The skull is from Poser 4, while the volumetric fire surrounding it was created by Menno (but is no longer available online). The books are from the Avalon archive of free meshes. The skull bookend was made using Amorphium, while the coffin and the Amulet of the Void were modelled in Cinema 4D GO. The stone block texture on the walls and the wood texture for the coffin were generated using POV-Ray, an amazing free raytracer.
