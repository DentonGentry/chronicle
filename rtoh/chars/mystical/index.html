Mystical

  Mystical is an Alu-Demon, the product of the union of a human male and a succubus. She was trained as an assassin in the service of Orcus, a position she fulfilled with great ability for several centuries. She grew to hate her employer, and was not saddened to see Orcus destroyed eight hundred years ago. Since then she has hunted down many of the demon lord's former minions, dispatching them with great relish.

  Mystical is a magehunter, a type of arcane spell user. Arcane magic comes naturally to her due to her demonic heritage. Her character sheet is available.
